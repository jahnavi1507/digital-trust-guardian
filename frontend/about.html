About the Project

Digital Trust Guardian is an AI-powered cybersecurity system designed to protect rural communities from cyber fraud, scams, and misinformation.

Problem Statement

Rural users face increasing cyber threats such as OTP fraud, fake loan schemes, deepfake scams, and identity misuse. Lack of awareness and tools makes them vulnerable.

Our Solution

  • AI-based scam detection
  • Voice alerts in local languages
  • Community-wide alert system
  • Deepfake detection
  • Works in low internet areas

Technology Used

  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Cybersecurity techniques

Impact

This system helps reduce financial fraud, builds trust in digital systems, and improves safety in rural communities.
